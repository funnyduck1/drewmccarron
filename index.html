About


Hello, my name is Drew McCarron. I am a graduate from Point Park University in Pittsburgh, PA. As a Cinema Production student, I was fortunate enough to work on several short films throughout my student career, with a strong focus on the post-production aspects of filmmaking, such as editing and sound. My passion for digital media production is longstanding, with extensive experience using photography and videography equipment, as well as owning and operating DJI drones. Personally, I describe myself as mature, respectful, friendly, and always loyal. In my spare time, I am passionate about music, film, and the arts. As an eagle scout, I also enjoy kayaking, hiking, and biking outdoors.
